Culture of South Africa

South Africa has 11 official languages and a rich mix of traditions, music, dance, and food cultures.

Languages

Zulu, Xhosa, Afrikaans, English and more.

Food

Braai, Bobotie, Biltong, Vetkoek.

Traditions

Music, dance, storytelling, and cultural ceremonies.
