Golden Lens Photography

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Golden Lens Photography

The name represents elegance, warmth, and professional quality photography. "Golden" symbolizes excellence, while "Lens" reflects the core tool of photography.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote a professional photography business by:

  • Showcasing photography work in an organized portfolio
  • Providing pricing information for photography packages
  • Sharing background information about the photographer
  • Allowing clients to submit booking inquiries through a contact form

Target Audience Scenarios

  • A bride planning her wedding wants to compare photography packages and view wedding portfolios.
  • A family looking for portrait photography wants to check prices and book a session.

Color Scheme

  • #C6A75E (Warm Gold) – Used for headings and buttons
  • #222222 (Charcoal Black) – Used for body text
  • #F8F8F8 (Soft White) – Main background
  • #EAEAEA (Light Gray) – Section backgrounds

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for headings
  • Poppins – Used for body text
