Site Name

South Africa Travel & Culture Guide

This website showcases South Africa's tourist attractions, cities, culture, food, wildlife, and travel information. The name clearly reflects the purpose of helping visitors learn about and explore South Africa.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide travelers and learners with useful information about South Africa. Visitors can discover popular destinations, learn about local culture and food, explore wildlife, check weather information, and contact the site for additional travel advice.

Website Pages

Home Page (index.html)

Introduces South Africa with a hero image, welcome message, featured destinations, travel tips, and links to the other pages.

Destinations (destinations.html)

Highlights major tourist destinations such as Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban, Table Mountain, and Kruger National Park.

Culture & Food (culture.html)

Provides information about South African traditions, languages, celebrations, music, and popular foods such as braai, bobotie, and biltong.

Wildlife & Weather (wildlife.html)

Features information about South Africa's wildlife, national parks, the Big Five animals, and seasonal weather conditions.

Contact (contact.html)

Contains a contact form where visitors can submit questions, comments, or travel inquiries.
