South Africa Travel & Culture Guide - Site Plan

Site Name

South Africa Travel & Culture Guide

This name clearly represents a travel-focused website dedicated to showcasing South Africa’s cities, culture, wildlife, food, and tourist attractions. It is designed to attract both local and international visitors.

Optional domain: southafricatravelguide.co.za

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide useful and engaging information about South Africa for tourists and learners. It will include travel guides, cultural insights, food highlights, wildlife information, weather details, and recommended destinations.

Scenarios

  • What are the best cities to visit in South Africa for first-time tourists?
  • What cultural experiences and traditions can visitors explore in South Africa?
  • What is the best time of year to visit South Africa for good weather and wildlife viewing?

Color Schema

These colors reflect South Africa’s natural landscapes and cultural richness:

Deep Green (#0b3d2e) – Used for headers, navigation, and emphasis

Gold (#d4af37) – Used for accents, highlights, and buttons

Sand (#f4f1ea) – Used for background color

Typography

Primary Font: Georgia (serif)

  • Headings: Georgia Bold
  • Body Text: Georgia Regular

This font choice reflects a classic, readable, and travel-magazine style aesthetic.

Wireframes

Mobile View

Mobile layout will stack content vertically: header, navigation, featured image, content sections, and footer.

Desktop View

Desktop layout will include a horizontal navigation bar, side-by-side content sections, and image grids.

Additional Notes

The website will be responsive using CSS media queries and will include:

  • Navigation menu across multiple pages
  • Image gallery of attractions and wildlife
  • Contact form for visitor inquiries
  • Travel tips and cultural facts sections
