🌍 South Africa Travel & Culture Guide

Website Planning Document

Site Name

South Africa Travel & Culture Guide

This name clearly describes the purpose of the website. It focuses on South Africa's travel destinations, culture, wildlife, food, and tourism. The title is easy to understand and helps visitors immediately know what information the site provides.

Optional Domain: southafricatravelguide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide visitors with useful information about South Africa's tourist attractions, cities, culture, wildlife, food, weather, and travel tips.

  • Popular tourist destinations
  • Information about major cities
  • South African culture and traditions
  • Traditional foods
  • Wildlife and national parks
  • Weather information
  • Travel tips
  • Contact form for visitor questions

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What are the best tourist attractions to visit in South Africa?

Scenario 2: What traditional South African foods should I try during my visit?

Scenario 3: What wildlife can I see in South Africa's national parks?

Scenario 4: What is the best time of year to visit South Africa?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #1B5E20 (Dark Green)

Used for headers, navigation bars, footers, and major headings.

Secondary Color: #F4A261 (Warm Orange)

Used for buttons, accents, highlights, and hover effects.

Neutral Color: #F8F9FA (Light Gray)

Used for page backgrounds and content sections.

Typography

Used for headings and navigation menus.

Open Sans

Used for body text, paragraphs, and forms.

Playfair Display

Used for special quotes and featured content sections.
