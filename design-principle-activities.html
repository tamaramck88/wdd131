Design Principles in Practice

Typography

Typography plays an important role in web design. Using readable fonts, proper spacing, and visual hierarchy improves user experience and accessibility.

Accessibility Tip

Always ensure sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds. This helps users with visual impairments read content more easily.

Consistency

Consistent colors, spacing, and layouts create a professional and predictable experience for users.
